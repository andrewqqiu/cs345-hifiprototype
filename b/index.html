Why Freecycle?
It helps you.

You can stop worrying about what to do with your
old items. Freecycling prevents that old couch from
taking up space in your garage, and saves you the
time of having to take it somewhere.

It helps others.

Something that’s no longer useful for you might still be useful for someone else! By Freecycling it, you can pass it on and help a stranger in need.

It helps the world.

Regular trash ends up in landfills, where it
helps destroy our environment and pollute
the planet. Freecycling stops that.
